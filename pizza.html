Pizza Dough

Serves 2

Prep Time 10 mins

Cook Time 20 mins

Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 cup self raising flour

Method

  1. Mix yoghurt and flour in a bowl until the mixture no longer sticks to your hands, adding more flour as needed, until the mixture is a dough.
  2. On a floured counter, roll dough into shape/size desired. Brush with oil on both sides.
  3. Cook in a pre-heated oven for 10-20 mins. For pizza it's recommended to put in oven about 10 mins before putting topping on.
